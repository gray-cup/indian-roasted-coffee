## About Coorg Arabica

Kodagu — known affectionately as Coorg — sits in the Western Ghats of Karnataka at elevations between 1,000 and 1,500 metres. The combination of misty mornings, rich laterite soil, and hand-picking at peak ripeness produces Arabica beans with an exceptionally clean, bright cup.

Our Coorg Arabica is washed-processed, which strips away the fruit layer quickly after harvest and lets the bean's inherent clarity shine through. Expect a light-medium body, a lively acidity, and layered flavour notes of jasmine, bergamot, and a lingering citrus finish.

## Flavour profile

- **Aroma:** Jasmine, green tea, light caramel
- **Taste:** Bright citrus (lemon zest, orange), floral honey
- **Finish:** Clean, sweet, with a delicate citrus tail
- **Body:** Light to medium — ideal for pour-over, Chemex, or Aeropress

## Best brewing methods

| Method | Grind | Ratio |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Pour-over | Medium-fine | 1 : 16 |
| Aeropress | Medium | 1 : 15 |
| Cold brew | Coarse | 1 : 8 |

## Sourcing & sustainability

Every lot comes from small family estates in and around the Madikeri and Virajpet taluks. Farmers are paid above Fair Trade floor prices, and the estates follow shade-grown practices that protect the native flora of the Western Ghats biodiversity hotspot.

## Storage

Store in an airtight container away from light and heat. Whole beans stay fresh for up to 30 days; once ground, use within 7 days for the best flavour.
