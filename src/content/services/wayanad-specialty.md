## About Wayanad Specialty Blend

Wayanad is a plateau district nestled in the Western Ghats on the Kerala–Karnataka border. Its climate — a tropical monsoon wet season followed by cool, dry winters — creates ideal conditions for a balanced blend of Arabica and Robusta that neither dominates nor disappears.

Our blend uses a 60/40 Arabica-to-Robusta ratio, honey-processed to retain just enough fruit mucilage on the bean. The result is a medium roast with the brightness and aroma of Arabica, the body and crema of Robusta, and a natural sweetness that needs no sugar.

## Flavour profile

- **Aroma:** Brown sugar, stone fruit, warm spice
- **Taste:** Smooth caramel, milk chocolate, a hint of cardamom
- **Finish:** Medium length, soft and slightly sweet
- **Body:** Medium-full — versatile across brew methods, outstanding as a morning cup

## Best brewing methods

| Method | Grind | Ratio |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Pour-over | Medium | 1 : 15 |
| Moka pot | Fine-medium | 1 : 7 |
| French press | Coarse | 1 : 14 |

## Sourcing & sustainability

Beans come from tribal and small-holder farmers in the Kalpetta and Mananthavady blocks of Wayanad. The estates are certified Rainforest Alliance and follow integrated pest management that avoids synthetic chemicals.

## Storage

Store in an airtight container away from light and heat. Whole beans stay fresh for up to 30 days after the roast date printed on the bag.
