question: How long do the beans stay fresh?
tags: [products]

esQuestion: ¿Cuánto tiempo se mantienen frescos los granos?
---

All our bags include a roast date on the label so you always know exactly how fresh your coffee is.

- **Light and medium roasts** — best within **30 days** of the roast date.
- **Dark roasts** — best within **45 days** of the roast date (the lower acidity means they hold up slightly longer).

Once you open the bag, transfer beans to an airtight container and store away from direct light and heat. Avoid the refrigerator — condensation is the enemy of coffee freshness.

For peak flavour, grind just before brewing.
