We roast to order, so your beans are roasted the morning after your order is placed and dispatched the same evening.

| Service | Estimated delivery |
|---------|-------------------|
| Standard (free above ₹1,500) | 4–6 business days |
| Express | 2–3 business days |
| Same-day (Bengaluru only) | Order before 10 a.m. |

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking link as soon as your parcel is dispatched. If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated window, contact us and we'll investigate right away.
