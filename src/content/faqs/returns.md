Because coffee is a perishable food product, we cannot accept returns of opened bags. However, we stand behind every roast:

- **Wrong item received** — contact us within 7 days and we'll send the correct product at no extra charge.
- **Damaged in transit** — send us a photo within 7 days; we'll arrange a replacement or refund.
- **Quality concern** — if your beans taste off or were clearly mis-roasted, reach out with details. We take every report seriously and will make it right.

Sealed, unopened bags may be returned within 14 days of delivery for a full refund.

To start a return or raise a concern, contact us by email at office@graycup.org or use the contact form on our site.
