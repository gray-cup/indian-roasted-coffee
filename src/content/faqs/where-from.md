All our beans are sourced directly from estates across four of India's premier coffee-growing regions:

- **Coorg (Kodagu), Karnataka** — high-altitude Arabica with bright, floral character
- **Chikmagalur, Karnataka** — legendary Robusta with full body and chocolate notes
- **Wayanad, Kerala** — lush highlands producing a balanced Arabica-Robusta blend
- **Nilgiri Hills, Tamil Nadu** — the highest-altitude lots with complex dark-fruit complexity

We work directly with farmers and estate managers, which means we can trace every batch to a specific location and harvest.
