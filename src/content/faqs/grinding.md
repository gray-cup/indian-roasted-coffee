**Whole beans are almost always the better choice** if you have a grinder — even an entry-level burr grinder makes a noticeable difference. Coffee begins to stale within minutes of being ground, so grinding fresh before each brew preserves the volatile aromatics that give each origin its character.

That said, pre-ground is a practical choice if you:
- Don't own a grinder
- Brew only one method (South Indian filter, for example, needs a very specific grind size)
- Travel frequently and want convenience

We offer pre-ground in coarse (French press), medium (pour-over), fine-medium (moka pot), and fine (espresso/filter) sizes. Select your grind on the product page before adding to your cart.
