It depends on how you brew and what flavours you enjoy:

- **Light-Medium** (Coorg Arabica) — if you like bright, fruity, or floral cups and prefer pour-over, Chemex, or Aeropress.
- **Medium** (Wayanad Specialty Blend) — if you want a balanced, easy-drinking coffee that works for everything from filter to moka pot.
- **Dark** (Chikmagalur Robusta, Nilgiri Estate Dark) — if you prefer a bold, full-bodied cup, South Indian filter coffee, or espresso with a thick crema.

Not sure? Start with the Wayanad Specialty Blend — it's our most versatile roast and a good introduction to Indian coffee.
