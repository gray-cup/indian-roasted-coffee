---
question: What makes Indian coffee special?
tags: [general]
order: 1
esQuestion: ¿Qué hace especial al café India?
---

India is one of the few countries where coffee is grown entirely under shade — a practice that slows bean development, deepens flavour complexity, and protects local biodiversity.

Indian coffee is also unique in that Robusta varieties grown here can rival the quality of Arabica from other origins, thanks to the altitude and the forest micro-climate. The result is a range of cups from delicately floral (Coorg Arabica) to boldly chocolatey (Chikmagalur Robusta) — all with a characteristic smoothness that sets Indian coffee apart.
